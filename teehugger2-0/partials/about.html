about us

TEEhuggers started out as a passion project for two former college roommates that blossomed into a lifestyle, philosophy, and guiding set of values. As consumers, we want to know that the money we pay for goods and services goes to responsible companies with sustainable business, management, and community practices.

As moms, wives, daughters, sisters, and neighbors, we know how important—and often scarce—quality and safety are in today’s crowded consumer goods market. That’s why we bring you TEEhugger products for you and your loved ones.

Designed, sourced, fabricated, manufactured, and distributed with high standards, respect for the environment and sustainability, and made for—and supported by—our wonderful community. We hope you love our products as much as we do!
